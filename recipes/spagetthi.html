Spaghetti

Italian curly food

Our favorite spaghetti is kid-friendly and made with a delicious homemade sauce making it the perfect family dinner recipe!

ingredients

  • ground beef
  • sauce
  • pasta

Steps

  1. Boil water and put the pasta
  2. Put the sauce on a pan with the ground beef and cook it there
  3. Drop the cooked pasta on the pan with the cook ground beef and mix it
  4. Thats it, now put it on a plate and eat it
